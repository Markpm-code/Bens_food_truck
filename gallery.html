A Culinary Fusion of Filipino and Irish Flavours

The Food Truck Images Gallery offers a vibrant glimpse into the world of mobile kitchens, highlighting the delicious fusion of Filipino and Irish cuisines. The gallery features tantalizing images of spring rolls, noodles, lugaw/Congee, and fried rice paired with fresh salads and delectable dessert, showcasing the rich and diverse flavours of the continent. Alongside these, the hearty comfort of Irish-inspired burgers, chicken goujons and chips adds a touch of European tradition to the mix.
